We are a non-profit website that lets near by homeless shelters document what resources they need and how high their person traffic is. We let the users of the website/app contact local shelters about a near by homeless person in need of help so the shelter near by can contact that person and possibly give them a place to stay. This app has been created for social good and to supply an easy access to information about homeless shelters.
